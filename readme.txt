== Description ==

WP with Spritz lets your readers read more of your content quicker than ever using Spritz patented speed reading technology.
  
Enable your readers to read and retain your content like never before! 

Major features in WP with Spritz include:
- Enable your viewers to read and retain your content faster than ever before.
- Works across any device, from desktops and laptops to tablets and smartphones.
- Enable or disable WP with Spritz on pages, posts or custom post types one by one or type by type as you prefer.
- Enable your viewers to easily collapse or dismiss the Spritzer to their preference.

PS: You'll need a Spritz Inc. API Key to use it. Keys are free for personal blogs; paid subscriptions are available for businesses and commercial sites.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-with-spritz.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Register for a Spritz Client ID at http://www.spritzinc.com/developers or email licensing@spritzinc.com
4. Input your Spritz Client ID in the Spritz Client ID field in the WP with Spritz settings
5. Select the content types you would like to enable the Spritzer on.
6. If you have a customized theme and want to manually control the content the Spritzer will read, edit the includes in the "Content Region Include" section of the plugin settings.
7. Customize other options as you like.
8. Don't forget to hit Save Changes!
9. Now your viewers can engage with your content through Spritz!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 1. What is "Spritzing?" =

Spritzing is an amazing new way to deliver content to readers so they can read more quickly without losing comprehension.  Learn more about Spritzing at http://www.spritzinc.com/the-science/

= 2. Why should I use WP with Spritz? =

Want to give your readers and option to engage with more of your content, more quickly?  There you go.

= 3. What if some of my readers like it, and some don't? =

WP with Spritz is easy to present and share with readers, but if a reader opts to close the Spritz reader it will remain closed throughout their tenure on your site unless they choose to re-open it.
